How NFW Works

NFW combines professional wrestling storytelling with blockchain ownership and community participation.

01

Mint Your Wrestler

Purchase a unique 1-of-1 NFW wrestler with their own identity, moveset, and backstory. Each wrestler is completely original.

02

Compete & Earn

Enter your wrestler into matches and tournaments to earn $PINCRED tokens, build their record, and climb the rankings.

03

Shape Their Journey

Influence your wrestler's career path, form alliances, develop rivalries, and make strategic decisions to increase their value.

04

Build Your Legacy

Win championships, create memorable moments, and establish your wrestler as a legend in the NFW universe.
