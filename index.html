About NFW

The world's first blockchain-powered wrestling federation where every fan becomes a stakeholder

Revolutionary Wrestling Experience

NFW combines the excitement of professional wrestling with cutting-edge blockchain technology. Own unique wrestler NFTs, participate in match predictions, and earn real rewards based on performance.

Our platform creates an immersive experience where fans aren't just spectators—they're active participants in shaping storylines and earning from their wrestling knowledge.

  • Own unique 1-of-1 wrestler NFTs
  • Earn $PINCRED tokens from matches
  • Predict match outcomes for rewards
  • Influence storylines and character development
  • Access exclusive merchandise and content
